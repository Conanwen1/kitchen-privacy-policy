Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import a recipe?

Tap the "+" button to create a new recipe, then select "Import from URL" or "Import from Text." You can paste a link from Instagram, TikTok, Xiaohongshu, or any recipe website. The app will automatically extract the recipe details for you.

How does audio extraction work?

For video-based recipes (TikTok, Xiaohongshu, etc.), the app can transcribe the audio from the video to extract recipe instructions. Tap the audio/microphone button when importing from a social media URL.

Can I import recipes in other languages?

Yes! The app automatically translates imported recipes to English, so you can import from any language.

How are calories and nutrition estimated?

The app uses AI to estimate calories and glycemic index (GI) for each ingredient. These are estimates and should not be used as a substitute for professional dietary advice.
