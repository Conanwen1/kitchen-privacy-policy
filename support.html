Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a recipe? Tap the "New Recipe" tab at the bottom of the screen. You can enter details manually, import from a URL, or use the AI-assisted import feature.
How do I save someone else's recipe? Open any recipe and tap the bookmark icon. Saved recipes appear in your "Saved" tab.
How do I delete my account? Go to the "My Recipes" tab, tap the ··· menu in the top-right corner, and select Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all your recipes.
How do I sign out? Tap the sign-out icon in the top-right corner of the Feed tab.
Can I edit or delete a recipe I published? Yes. Go to "My Recipes", open your published recipe, and use the edit or delete options available there.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? On the login screen, use the "Forgot Password" option (if available) or contact us at kitchen.app.support@gmail.com for assistance.
